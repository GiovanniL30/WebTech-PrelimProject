ADVOCACY

The Global Coral Reef Alliance (GCRA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting and preserving coral reefs worldwide. Coral reefs, often referred to as "rainforests of the sea," provide habitat for a vast array of marine life and protect coastlines from erosion and storm damage. However, these ecosystems are facing unprecedented threats from climate change, pollution, and overfishing, with an estimated 30% of coral reefs already destroyed.


The GCRA works to address these issues through conservation efforts, research, and education, promoting sustainable reef management and supporting local communities that depend on these ecosystems. By taking action to protect coral reefs, we can help maintain the health of our oceans, support biodiversity, and ensure the long-term livelihoods of millions of people around the world.
