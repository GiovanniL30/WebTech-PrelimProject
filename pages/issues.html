Issues and Solutions

Overfishing

Overfishing in the Philippines has been a problem due to the fact that over 75% of the nation’s fishing ground has been depleted. This threatens the nation’s food source and the livelihoods of many within the Philippines.

Solution:

Regulation and limitation of fishing within Philippine waters to allow fishes to breed and multiply. A movement called Fish Forever also aims to help fishermen manage and regulate fishing within Philippine waters.
